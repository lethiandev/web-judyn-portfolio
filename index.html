Brand Book

Price: from £300
Preparation of the brand book includes:

  • Meaning of the symbol – an explanation of the logo design vision, its meaning, and how the brand character is emphasized
  • Construction of the logo – technical representation of the creation of the logo
  • Protective field of the logo – indicating a free area around the logo so that it remains legible and attractive to the audience
  • Colour scheme of the logo – the precise colors used in the logo
  • Examples of logo usage – visualizations of materials on which the brand logo will be eventually placed
